Seals for Hydraulics & Pneumatics

A pioneer in design, development, manufacturing and marketing of high-performance sealing systems and solutions for all types of hydraulic and pneumatic applications.

Sakhashri Seals Corporation delivers precision-engineered sealing solutions for industries including construction, mining, agriculture, and material handling.

Our seals are manufactured using high-performance materials like Polyurethane, PTFE, NBR Rubber, and advanced polymers ensuring durability, efficiency, and long service life.

We are committed to providing reliable, customized sealing solutions that meet modern industrial demands.
