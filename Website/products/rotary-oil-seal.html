Rotary Oil Seals in Pune

Rotary Shaft Seals | Single & Double Lip | All Industries | Pune & Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation is a leading manufacturer and supplier of Rotary Oil Seals in Pune. These seals, also known as shaft seals or lip seals, are used to retain lubrication and prevent contamination in rotating shaft assemblies found in gearboxes, pumps, motors, and axles.

Our rotary seals are engineered with precision-ground sealing lips that ensure tight contact with the rotating shaft, providing leak-free performance even at high shaft speeds and pressures.
