Manufacturer | Supplier | Dealer | Trader — Pune, PCMC, Bhosari, Chakan & Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation is the leading manufacturer and supplier of Industrial Oil Seals, Silicon Oil Seals, and Rubber Oil Seals in Pune, PCMC, Bhosari, Chakan, Talwade, Ranjangaon, Hadapsar, Shirwal, Nashik, Nagpur, Kolhapur, Bangalore, and Pirangut.

Oil seals are essential components used to protect mechanical equipment from damage by preventing the unwanted escape of fluids such as oil or grease. Rubber or metal-cased oil seals are the most frequently utilised in the majority of applications and play a vital role in the proper functioning of machinery.

We provide top-quality oil seals engineered to fit your specific needs — reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions that enhance the performance and lifespan of your machinery.

Properties of Industrial Oil Seals

  • 1
    Prevents Leakage: Oil seals act as a barrier that prevents fluids from leaking out of the machinery or equipment, which can lead to damage and reduced performance.
  • 2
    Reduces Maintenance Costs: Using high-quality oil seals reduces the need for repairs and maintenance — saving your business cost in the long run.
  • 3
    Improves Efficiency: When machinery is protected from leakage, performance is optimized, resulting in higher efficiency and productivity.
  • 4
    Extends Lifespan: Oil seals provide a protective layer against harmful elements and contaminants like dust, moisture, and debris — extending machine life.
