O Rings in Pune

Manufacturer | Supplier — NBR, Viton, Silicone, EPDM O-Rings | Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation is a trusted supplier of high-quality O-Rings in Pune, Bhosari, Chakan, and across India. O-rings are the most widely used sealing elements in both static and dynamic applications, offering simple yet reliable sealing solutions for hydraulic, pneumatic, and industrial use.

Our O-rings are manufactured from premium materials including NBR, Viton, Silicone, and EPDM to ensure compatibility with various fluids, temperatures, and pressures. We supply standard and custom-sized O-rings to match any groove dimension.
