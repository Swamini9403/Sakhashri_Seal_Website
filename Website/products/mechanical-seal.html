Mechanical Seals in Pune

Pump & Compressor Mechanical Seals | Manufacturer & Supplier | Pune, Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation supplies precision-engineered Mechanical Seals in Pune for pumps, compressors, mixers, and agitators. A mechanical seal is a device that joins systems or mechanisms together by preventing leakage, containing pressure, or excluding contamination through the use of precisely lapped sealing faces.

Our mechanical seals are available in single, double, and cartridge configurations to meet the specific requirements of different industrial applications and process fluids.
