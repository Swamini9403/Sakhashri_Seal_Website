Piston Glyd Rings in Pune

PTFE Piston Seals | Double-Acting Hydraulic Cylinders | Manufacturer & Supplier

Sakhashri Seals Corporation manufactures and supplies Piston Glyd Rings — a combination of a PTFE sliding ring energized by an NBR or PU O-ring — designed for double-acting piston sealing in hydraulic cylinders. These seals offer superior performance compared to conventional elastomeric piston seals.

The PTFE sliding ring provides an extremely low friction coefficient while the elastomeric energizer generates the required sealing force. This combination results in excellent sealing performance with minimal breakout friction.
