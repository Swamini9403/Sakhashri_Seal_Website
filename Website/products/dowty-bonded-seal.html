Dowty Bonded Seals in Pune

Bonded Sealing Washers | Hydraulic Fittings, Port Plugs & Banjo Bolts | Pune & Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation supplies Dowty Bonded Seals — also known as bonded washers or bonded sealing washers — in Pune and across India. A Dowty seal consists of a metal washer with an elastomeric seal bonded to its inner diameter. When tightened, the rubber is compressed and conforms to both mating faces, providing a positive, leak-free seal.

These seals are widely used for hydraulic port plugs, banjo bolts, pipe unions, and threaded fittings in automotive, construction, agriculture, and industrial machinery applications.
