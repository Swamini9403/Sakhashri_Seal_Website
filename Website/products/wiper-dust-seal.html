Wiper Dust Seals in Pune

Hydraulic Rod Wiper / Scraper Seals | PU, NBR, PTFE | Manufacturer & Supplier

Sakhashri Seals Corporation manufactures and supplies high-quality Wiper Dust Seals in Pune. Also known as scraper seals or exclusion seals, these components are installed at the outer end of a hydraulic cylinder to wipe off dirt, dust, moisture, and other contaminants from the rod as it retracts into the cylinder.

Effective wiper seals are essential to protect internal cylinder seals and extend the overall life of the hydraulic system. Our wipers are available in single-lip and double-lip configurations with both metric and inch sizes.
