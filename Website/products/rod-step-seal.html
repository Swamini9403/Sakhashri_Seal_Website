Rod Step Seals in Pune

Step Seals for Hydraulic Cylinder Rods | High-Pressure Sealing | Pune & Pan India

Sakhashri Seals Corporation supplies high-quality Rod Step Seals designed for superior sealing in hydraulic cylinder rod applications. The step seal design combines a PTFE or PU sliding ring with an elastomeric energizer, offering low friction, excellent pressure resistance, and outstanding performance even under extreme conditions.

Also known as compact seals or step-cut seals, these are preferred in applications where minimal installation space is available and high pressure must be contained effectively.
