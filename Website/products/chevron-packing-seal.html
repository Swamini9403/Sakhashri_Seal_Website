Chevron Packing Seals in Pune

V-Ring Packing Sets | Adjustable Multi-Lip Sealing | Hydraulic & Pneumatic Applications

Sakhashri Seals Corporation supplies high-quality Chevron Packing Seals (also called V-Packing or V-Ring Seals) in Pune. These are stacked V-shaped seal sets that provide adjustable sealing pressure through a gland nut, making them ideal for reciprocating rod and piston applications in pumps, valves, and cylinders operating at high pressure.

The number of rings in the stack can be adjusted to achieve the desired sealing performance and service life. Available in NBR, PTFE, leather, and fabric-reinforced configurations.

Key Properties

  • 1
    Adjustable Sealing Force: Gland nut allows field adjustment of sealing pressure to compensate for wear.
  • 2
    Very High Pressure: Handles pressures up to 700 bar — among the highest of any rod seal type.
  • 3
    Versatile Materials: Available in rubber, leather, fabric, and PTFE to suit any process fluid.
  • 4
    Long Packing Life: Multi-ring stack distributes pressure load, significantly extending service intervals.
