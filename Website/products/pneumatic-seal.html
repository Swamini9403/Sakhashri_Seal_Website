Pneumatic Seals in Pune

Air Cylinder Sealing | Rod, Piston & Wiper | Manufacturer & Supplier | Pune

Sakhashri Seals Corporation is a leading supplier of Pneumatic Seals in Pune, designed to provide efficient sealing for air-powered cylinders and pneumatic actuators. These seals prevent air leakage in both rod and piston positions, ensuring optimal cylinder performance, smooth operation, and energy efficiency.

Our pneumatic seals are manufactured using polyurethane and rubber materials that offer exceptional low-friction characteristics — critical for high-speed pneumatic applications where stick-slip motion must be eliminated.
